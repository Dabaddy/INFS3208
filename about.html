Why Deep Speech?

Advances in Machine Learning had lead to a development of “Fake News” through the use of deep fakes: replication of human traits using artificial intelligence.
With Project Purple we intend to use neural text to speech models which will attempt to replicate human voice directly from the text and the voice recording provided. With an increased understanding of the potential threats that could occur as a result of targeted Deep Fake attacts we could implement a solution building up on the neural network concepts.
